What Guides Us

Three things
we never compromise on.

01

An Anglican foundation

Under the Diocese of Manicaland, chapel life and pastoral care are woven into every term — not an add-on, but the spine of the school.

02

The ZIMSEC curriculum, taken seriously

Sciences, Commercials, and Arts, taught by qualified staff, producing the kind of pass rates that put us 38th in Manicaland in 2025.

03

A real boarding community

Boreholes, solar pumps, and a working farm project feed a dining hall that serves students from across Zimbabwe — discipline learned, not lectured.
